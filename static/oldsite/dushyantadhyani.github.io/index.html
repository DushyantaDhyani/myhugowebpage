Dushyanta would be joining the Masters in Computer Science program at the Computer Science Department, Ohio State University.

He worked as a Research Associate at the Precog Research Group @ IIIT-Delhi under the supervision of Dr. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru. He was working in an interesting domain of Crime Analytics.

Prior to that, he worked as a Research Assistant at the Ubiquitous Knowledge Processing Lab, TU Darmstadt. under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Iryna Gurevych. He worked on the use of Graphical Models for Event/Participant extraction from Natural Language Text.

He has also had short stints in the industry and entrepreneurial initiatives.

Dushyanta is a Machine Learning enthusiast, interested in both theory and applications. Two broad application domains that specifically catch his attention are NLP and Vision. His interests also lie in Theoretical aspects of ML, Deep Learning, Convex optimization and Algorithms.

He completed his Bachelor's in Information Technology from National Institute of Technology,Kurukshetra .
